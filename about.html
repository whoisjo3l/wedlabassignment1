About Us

 
 
GK Figure was incorporated as GK Figure Pte. Ltd. (UEN: 202040453R) in Malaysia
 
GK Figure was born and started as a small scale home-based eCommerce in Singapore. Today we are operating one of the most popular online Figurines and Collectibles stores that Collectors worldwide trusted. Our orders are shipped directly from our Distributor or Manufacturer throughout the world.
 
As a Collector ourselves, we understand the hardship of getting your beloved Figurines or Collectibles when it is not made available at your Country of Residence. With this in mind, we have decided to provide you with a wide selection of products in our catalog that you can choose to purchase. And we will settle all the rest for you. \ All you have to do is sit back and relax, while waiting for your favorite Figurines or Collectibles delivered to your doorstep.
 
GK Figure is tirelessly sourcing for the latest and trendiest products for our Collectors worldwide from our Distributors and Manufacturers. Without the cost of traditional brick and mortar store, we pass on the savings to the Collectors like you.
 
Our love with Figurines or Collectibles and passion to serve has brought us to who we are today. We always believe that all the Collectors deserve to get their beloved Figurines or Collectibles regardless of where they live and at an affordable price.
